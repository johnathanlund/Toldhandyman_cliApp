    ABOUT US

    Told Handyman Service is a family owned company, built to satisfy its customers needs. Told Handyman is owned by Dave and Belinda Told, who also own and operate Told Plumbing. The company was born out of seeing a need amongst their customers for more help then just plumbing needs, but not knowing where to turn. So they set out to fill that need with trusted quality workmanship, with a focus on customer satisfaction every time.

    Meet Our Handyman

    Born in Ohio, grew up in California, Marlin started working at the age of 16 in a plastic factory. In 1989 he moved to Utah and started remodeling homes until he got a job with Stouffers as a professional maintenance mechanic, repairing their machines. Four years later he earned his contractors license and helped run an HVAC business. He shifted his specialty back to home repairs after some years, and began doing home warranty repairs for Trophy Homes. Then in 2009 Marlin was approached by Dave Told with a business idea, and he has been the face and life of Told Handyman ever since. Doing everything from home remodels, to doing small repairs and improvements, there’s no job too small for Told Handyman.

    Told Plumbing

    Told Plumbing is a family owned business, founded and operated by David and Belinda Told. David and Belinda are 4th generation Pleasant Grove residents. David was raised on the family farm that remains in operation today. Belinda's family comes from a long line of successful business owners. David began plumbing in 1980 and has worked all along the Wasatch Front. David and Belinda attribute their success to quality employees and hard work.
